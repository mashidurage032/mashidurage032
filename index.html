01 — about

I care about how things look just as much as how they work.

I'm currently studying for my Higher National Diploma in Information Technology at the Sri Lanka Advanced Technology Institute (SLIATE), Dehiwala — building my skills in full-stack web development, though front-end and UI/UX are where I genuinely light up. I like taking a rough idea and shaping it into something people actually enjoy clicking through.

Most of what I've built so far lives in the travel and outdoor space — a hiking gear platform and a tourism marketplace currently in progress. I'm drawn to projects connected to travel and nature, so building products for that world doesn't feel like work.

Outside of code, I'm probably planning a hike or thinking about the next place I want to explore.
